Welcome to Orbital Insights Ltd

Orbital Insights Ltd is a New Zealand–based organization dedicated to developing compact satellites for environmental and climate research. Our mission is to make high-quality Earth observation data available to researchers, educators, and communities through open, transparent systems.

Abstract

This site presents the final interface for a commercial web presence for Orbital Insights Ltd. It demonstrates consistent navigation, professional style, accessibility, and JavaScript features such as a live clock and a dynamic data dashboard to showcase telemetry concepts.

Why Our Work Matters

Our satellites support climate science by monitoring atmospheric change, polar ice, and ocean dynamics. Open data and clear visualizations help governments, universities, and the public make informed environmental decisions.
