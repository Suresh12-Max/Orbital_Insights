Company Details & Introduction

Founded in 2023 in Auckland, Orbital Insights Ltd designs affordable small-satellite payloads and ground-station telemetry for climate research and education. We champion open data and collaboration.

What We Do

  • Design & integration of nano-satellite payloads
  • Telemetry and ground communication systems
  • Data processing & visualization platforms
  • Education & STEM outreach in NZ schools

Our Satellite

InsightSat-1 is a micro-satellite targeting LEO (~505 km). Sensors include optical, infrared, and environmental monitors for temperature and greenhouse gas proxies. Data is released under an open license for research and teaching.
